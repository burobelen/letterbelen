Belén beyond 2025

We are excited to share an evolution at buroBelén as we embark on a new chapter of growth, innovation, and purpose. Founded in 2012 by Brecht Duijf and Lenneke Langenhuijsen, Belén has flourished over the past decade as a pioneering design studio known for its unique approach to material research and multidisciplinary collaboration. With a team of designers, interior architects, and skilled makers, we have developed long-term partnerships with architects, craftspeople, scientists, and industry leaders across a wide array of projects.

Our collaborations with respected groups amongst others tapamakers Tonga group, V8 Architects, Studioilse, Rigo, the Institute für Textilchemie und Textilphysik, and the hospitality experts Aedes Real Estate as well as institutions like MoMA New York, Stedelijk Museum Amsterdam and Boijmans van Beuningen Rotterdam, have contributed to a rich tapestry of design that combines innovation with cultural depth. As we move forward, this spirit of collaboration will continue to shape our projects.
  
Starting in 2025, after ten beautiful years at Belén, Brecht Duijf will embark on new adventures, we thank her for the meaningful role in establishing and developing Belén and wish her luck on her future journey. Read more on Duijf’s future endeavours in the letter below. 

​Lenneke Langenhuijsen will continue helming buroBelén independently, guiding the studio into a new era focused on creating soulful, tactile environments. Our commitment to balance the material and immaterial, shaping spaces that are as spiritually resonant as they are functionally engaging. Leading to interiors for hotels, sacred architecture, and the revitalization of monumental buildings in need of new programming. Where historical context merges seamlessly with material innovations, acoustic comfort, and a deep sense of place.


Belén will continue to deepen its research and development efforts, nurture design talent, and cultivate new partnerships with experienced professionals. Working in consortiums with leading experts and producers, the studio will expand its consultancy offerings, ensuring our projects are not only functionally engaging but spiritually resonant.

We are grateful for the support of our partners, clients, and community as we undertake this inspiring new phase, and we invite you to be part of it. Together, we will create environments that honour history, embrace innovation, and bring lasting beauty to spaces worldwide.

Thank you for your ongoing trust and collaboration. Visit our updated website at www.burobelen.com

Enjoy the last month of the year.

 

Warm regards,

Lenneke Langenhuijsen and the team of Belén


Dear friends of Belén,

 

After ten wonderful years as co-director of buroBelén, the time has come for me to take a new direction in my personal and professional journey. It has been an absolute privilege to collaborate with so many of you and to be part of your creative processes, achievements, exhibitions and collections over the past years. Our shared work has always been a constant source of inspiration, and I am deeply grateful for the trust and collaborations we have built together. 

 

Over the past year, I have dedicated myself to spiritual development, which has opened new paths for exploration and expression. As a designer, this has provided me with a more holistic and introspective perspective. Color remains central to my practice. Additionally, sound has come to play an important role.

 

I believe that design can serve as a conduit to deeper connection and transformation towards more morally and culturally productive futures. My interest and intuition are focused on creating healing spaces, spiritual experiences, and multidimensional photography, and I look forward to sharing this next chapter with you as I continue to grow. If you would like to stay updated on my journey, you can follow me on Instagram @brechtduijf.

 

Thank you for your support and collaboration over the years. It has been an honor to work with you, and I hope our paths will cross again in the future.

 

I wish you all the best in your continued endeavors.

 

With warm regards,
Brecht Duijf
